About Us

Our team, which has accumulated its experience in the field of test measurement devices and defense industry since 2012, established Pentamek Defense in 2017 in order to serve the Turkish defense industry and electronics world. Pentamek continues to serve the industry in order to reduce the foreign dependency of our country, to find solutions to logistics and supply problems, and to lead in the field of engineering and design. We have made it our duty to meet the needs of the sector with our experience, supplier and customer relations in the field of defense industry. PENTAMEK, which closely follows the developing technology; has adopted a continuous development approach and customer-oriented work as a company culture.
